Planet

Game

About

  • Welcome to Planet Game! In this thrilling adventure, you're tasked with protecting Earth from invading aliens and zombies. As the player, your mission is clear: pilot your spaceship armed with powerful weapons and navigate through space to eliminate the enemies on earth.
  • Your goal is to destroy aliens and zombies before they have a chance to devour our planet. With each enemy you defeat, you'll earn points that not only increase your score but also replenish the health of both you and Earth. But remember, if Earth's health drops to zero, it's game over!

How To Play

  • Navigate your spaceship across the screen using the W, A, S, and D keys.
  • Use the space bar to fire bullets and combat incoming enemies.
  • Beware, aliens will also fire back when you're within their range.
  • Keep moving, keep shooting, and defend Earth from the alien invasion!
  • Stay close to Earth to avoid getting lost in space.
